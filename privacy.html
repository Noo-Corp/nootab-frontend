Noo Tab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Overview
Noo Tab does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All data used by the application stays in your browser (local storage or cookies).

Google Services
If you connect your Google account to view Gmail or Calendar data, authorization is handled securely via Google OAuth. Noo Tab does not store your credentials or data from your Google account. All access happens locally in your browser.

Analytics
We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with Noo Tab. This includes anonymized information such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, and general location (based on IP). This data helps us improve the product and is never used for advertising.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using browser settings or Google's opt-out tool.

Data Sharing
We do not sell or share any personal data. Analytics data is handled by Google under their Privacy Policy.

Your Control
You may clear all local data stored by Noo Tab via the Clear Data button or your browser settings at any time.
