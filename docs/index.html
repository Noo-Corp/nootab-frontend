Welcome to Noo Tab!

Adding, Removing, and Editing Events

Click a day on the calendar.

Enter event details.

Click "Save" to add or update an event.

Click "Delete" to remove all events from a day.

Changing the Calendar View

Choose a month and year from the dropdown menus.

Click "Show" to switch to the chosen month and year.

Adding and Removing Bookmarks

Click the plus button at the top of the page.

Click on the "+ New" bookmark and enter the URL.

(Optional) Add a name to your bookmark.

Click the minus button next to a bookmark to remove it.

Opening Bookmarks

Click a bookmark to open it in the current tab.

Open all bookmarks in separate tabs by clicking "Open All".

Your browser may block multiple tabs from opening the first time you use the "Open All" feature.

Allow popups from nootab.com in the prompt that appears if you want the "Open All" feature to work fully.

Navigation

Switch between calendar and notes view using the tabs at the bottom of the screen or by pressing the tab key.

Notes

Take notes using the notes tab and save them securely by clicking the save button or using the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+S.

Your data will be stored locally in your browser.

Colour Wheel

Click to change the site's colour theme.

Dark Mode Toggle

Use the icon to switch between dark and light mode.
