Welcome to Noo Tab

Noo Tab is a customizable new tab page designed for productivity. Add the tools you need — notes, checklists, trackers, and more — in modular panels that run independently.

Getting started

To add your first panel, click the red square with a plus (+), or use the "Panels" button in the top right. You can add, remove, and rearrange panels at any time.

Panel buttons

There are two buttons at the bottom of each panel:

⤷ Privacy button to toggle the panel's visibility

❏ Opens an expanded view of the panel with additional features

Managing your data

All data is stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent or synced externally. Use the "Data" button in the top right to download, upload, or clear your data whenever you like.

Warning: Clearing your cookies or history will erase your Noo Tab data. Download your data first.
